## Signs You Need Emergency Service

- Sewage backing up into fixtures
- Strong sewage odors inside the home
- Pooling water around tank or drainfield

## What To Do First

1. Stop water usage
2. Keep people and pets away from affected areas
3. Call a licensed emergency septic provider
4. Ventilate affected spaces if safe

## FAQs

### Will emergency pumping fix the problem permanently?

Pumping relieves pressure and stops the overflow. Further inspection and repairs may be needed to address root causes.
