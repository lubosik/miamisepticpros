## Why Septic Inspections Matter in Tampa

Inspections catch issues early, prevent costly surprises during home sales, and ensure compliance with county health requirements.

## What Inspectors Check

1. Tank access, structure, and baffles
2. Sludge and scum levels (pumping recommendations)
3. Drainfield performance indicators
4. Inlet/outlet pipe condition
5. Optional camera inspection for diagnostics
