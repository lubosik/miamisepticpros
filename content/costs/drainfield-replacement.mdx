## Drainfield Replacement Costs

Drainfield replacement is a major expense, typically costing **$10,000–$25,000** for residential systems. This investment is necessary when your existing drainfield has failed and can't be repaired.

## Cost Breakdown

| Factor | Cost Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Standard replacement | $10,000–$15,000 | Most residential systems (1,000 sq ft) |
| Large system | $15,000–$25,000 | Larger homes or commercial (2,000+ sq ft) |
| Alternative system | $18,000–$30,000 | Aerobic systems required in some areas |

## Factors Affecting Cost

- Soil conditions and percolation rates
- Size of the required drainfield
- Local permitting and engineering requirements
- Equipment access and excavation difficulty
- Whether an alternative system is required
