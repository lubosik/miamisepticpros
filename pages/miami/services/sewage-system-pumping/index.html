What sewage system pumping involves

Professional pumping follows structured process:

Step 1: Location and access

Technician locates tank on property. Accesses tank through access ports or risers. Prepares work area. Sets up equipment. Ensures safe access.

Step 2: Assessment

Assesses overall tank condition. Measures scum and sludge levels. Inspects tank structure. Notes any damage or issues. Determines pumping needs.

Step 3: Waste removal

Uses high-capacity vacuum truck. Removes all accumulated waste. Pumps liquids and solids. Ensures complete removal. Typically completes within one hour.

Step 4: Tank cleaning

Rinses tank thoroughly. Removes remaining debris. Cleans tank walls. Ensures clean surface. Prepares for inspection.

Step 5: System inspection
