Drainage System Repairs in Miami, FL

Drainage system repairs in Miami typically cost $300-$700 for routine maintenance, $500-$2,500 for pipe repairs, $1,000-$5,000 for catch basin repairs, and $2,000-$10,000 for extensive system repairs depending on scope and complexity (Miami-Dade Permits). Blockage and accumulation of sediment and debris in drainage systems are leading causes of flooding in Miami-Dade County, with grass clippings, leaves, debris, and excessive lawn care products blown into stormwater inlets causing system failures.

Common signs drainage systems need repair include standing water after rainfall, visible flooding during average rain events, clogged storm drains with visible debris, sinkholes or subsidence around drainage structures, foul odors from drains, and foundation water intrusion (EPA). Drainage repair process includes field inspection of existing systems, pipe desilting to inspect joints and identify structural deficiencies, video inspection where access allows, coordination with drainage and maintenance offices, and comprehensive summary report of findings.

Professional drainage repair ensures proper diagnostic assessment using video inspection, compliance with FDOT drainage design standards, coordination with multiple agencies, adequate maintenance access for future repairs, and long-term system reliability preventing cascading failures.

Repair vs replacement

Replacement needed when:
  • Extensive corrosion and deterioration
  • Multiple structural failures
  • Pressure rating requirements exceed 10 psi
  • System design inadequate for needs
